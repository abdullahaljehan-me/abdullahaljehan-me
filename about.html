Who I Am

I'm an engineering aspirant from Dhaka, Bangladesh. Science and mathematics are where I think clearly—physics especially. Engineering is where I'm heading, starting from the ground up: C programming, Linux systems, and embedded hardware like the ESP32.

I'm not taking the typical path. Instead of jumping straight to web development, I'm building a solid foundation in systems programming and understanding how computers actually work at the hardware level.

Currently, I'm a Founding Advisor at Kynatium Labs, where we're making tech fundamentals less intimidating for students in Bangladesh. I help shape strategy, review technical content, and guide our community outreach efforts.
